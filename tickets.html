Exhibition Ticket Design (2021)

I chose to work on two exhibition prompts with different themes.

The Prompt

I wanted to create ticket designs for two exhibitions with very contrasting themes and aesthetic. For my "Alkaline" exhibition, I was heading towards a more vibrant and abstract concept. Meanwhile for the "Red Moon" exhibition, I wanted to approach it with a minimalistic taste.

Essential Information

  • Exhibition Name
  • Exhibition Run Time
  • Exhibition Hours
  • Exhibition Description
  • Artist Name
  • Adult, Child, or Senior Ticket
  • Barcode or Qr Code
  • Location

Alkaline Style Guide

  • Heading
  • Telephone Size 72
  • SubHeading
  • Roboto Bold Size 24
  • Body Text
  • Roboto Light Size 24

From Sketch to Finished Image

Inspired by Matisse, I chose to go for a more vibrant route. The concept for the design was a woman in the center surrounded by a garden filled with an abundance of fruit. Blues, orange, yellows, and pink decorate the design.
