Hello! Welcome to my page!

I’m a current sophomore studying IMA (Interactive Media Arts) at NYU Tisch. Growing up between two cities/states: Beijing and California, I absorbed different content which influenced a lot of creations. Having a background in fine arts, I’m constantly trying to combine my creativity with new technical skills I’ve learned in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. As an artist, I aim to create works that walk the fine line of evoking emotions and providing a message. If you’d like to know my about my experiences, definitely check out my resume.
