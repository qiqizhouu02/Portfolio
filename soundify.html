A music platform that builds community.

Survey for the Listeners

On my Instagram, I created a poll asking users what music platform they used or used the most often. The survey recieved an overwhelming response with 301 respondents, and 244 people chose Spotify as their most used music platform.

My Demographic

Using my instagram insights, I was able to better understand the age range and location of my audience.

Unique Features

Using the feedback and answers I received through my surveys and interviews, I decided to create unique features to create a further immersive experience.

In-App Communication

Posting on Listener Hubs Artists Chatting Listeners Direct Message
To promote community and bring people together, there are three main methods for users to communicate. Listeners can chat with each other through the DM function or post on the “Hubs“ to reach a larger group. Smaller artists can chat with each other with the DM function too— allowing them to recieve feedback and make changes to songs they are working on.

Ambient Noise

With just a click of a button, users have the choice of rain, thunder, ocean, and other nature sounds to play in the background of their songs.
