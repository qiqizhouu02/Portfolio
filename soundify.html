Soundify App Design (2021)

A music platform that builds community.

The Prompt

In my Critical Experience class. I was given the topic of “copyright“ I was assigned to create a work that took upon a critical lens about “copyright“. From there I decided to create a music platform centered around community.

Team

Duration

1.5 Weeks

1

Time to get to know the music streaming world!

As an avid music listener, I knew what I wanted from music platform. However, I wanted to know what an artist would want from one. To under my demographic, I decided to do a survey and then interview some individuals.
  • Questions:
  • 1. What are the pros and cons of these music platforms?
  • 2. What features do listeners and artists want?
  • 3. What is my community's demographic?
    •

Survey for the Listeners

On my Instagram, I created a poll asking users what music platform they used or used the most often. The survey recieved an overwhelming response with 301 respondents, and 244 people chose Spotify as their most used music platform.

My Demographic

Using my instagram insights, I was able to better understand the age range and location of my audience.

The Interview Results

To go more depth, I decided to interview some listeners and artists to get their take on their favorite music platforms. This way I knew how I would design the interface and what features to add to the app.
After interviewing them, I had a relative idea of what features I wanted to add to the app. More so, I needed to make an interface that could ecnompass all these features.
